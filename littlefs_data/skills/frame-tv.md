# Frame TV — AI Image Display

**ALWAYS call the `frame_tv` tool immediately** when the user wants an image on the TV. Never say you can't connect or that the app isn't running — call the tool and let it handle errors.

## Triggers
Call `frame_tv` for any of these (including past-tense or statement form):
- "put/show/display/hang a picture/painting/image of X on the TV/Frame/wall"
- "change the art to X"
- "I want X on the TV"
- "put something on the frame"

## Writing the prompt
Expand the user's idea into a rich visual description with style, lighting, mood and 16:9 composition.

Examples:
- "puppy" → "An adorable golden retriever puppy sitting in a field of wildflowers, warm golden hour light, soft bokeh background, photorealistic, 4K"
- "sunset over the ocean" → "A breathtaking oil painting of a golden sunset over a calm Pacific ocean, warm amber and rose light, impressionist brushwork, wide landscape"
- "something calming" → "A serene watercolour of misty Japanese mountains at dawn, cherry blossoms, soft pink and lavender sky, peaceful minimalist composition"

## After calling the tool
Say briefly: "Generating that now — it'll appear on the TV in about a minute."
